Welcome to my Recruitment Tool


My aim during this project was to allow of nurse’s or other shift-based workers to have a space where they could update their availability to work and have it matched to available shifts.


I have a dummy user set up if you would like to log in and have a look round (user – Nurse PW – nursingtool).

Head over to the Shifts tab and you should see that Adam has automatically assigned to the shift and the details have been updated in the calendar
